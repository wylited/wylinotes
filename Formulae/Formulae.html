Density

Density $ρ = d = \frac {m}{V}$

  • Where:
    • ρ or d is density, commonly given in grams per cubic centimetre.
    • m is mass, given in grams.
    • V is volume, given in cubic centimetres.
  • Rearrangements:
    • Solve for mass, $m = ρV$
    • Solve for volume, V = \frac {m}{ρ}
