GPE and KE

Objectives: Describe and explain GPE and KE. Know the equations for both energy types.

Equation for weight

Weight (N) = Mass (Kg) * Gravitational acceleration (ms-2). Gravitational force earth is usually used as 10 ms-2 or 9.8 ms-2.

Equation for GPE, Gravitational potential energy.

GPE (Joules) = Mass (Kg) * Gravitational acceleration (ms-2) * Height (Meters)

Equation for KE, Kinetic Energy

KE (Joules)= Mass (Kg) x Velocity (ms-2)

Equation for Speed.

Speed (m/s) = Distance (m) / Time (s)

Car experiment.

Measure the time it takes for a 1 kg car to travel down a 1.5 meter ramp. Gravity = 10 m/s
