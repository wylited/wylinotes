There are **4** major types of numeral systems. (major as in they are actually used)

- [[Binary]] (Base 2)
- [[Octal]] (Base 8)
- [[Decimal]] (Base 10)
- [[Hexadecimal]] (Base 16)

Each of them have their own pro's and con's, explained further in their sub-notes.
