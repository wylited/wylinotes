# GPE (Gravitational Potential Energy)

$GPE = mgh$

## Where:

- Mass (m) is in KG
- Gravity (g) is in meters / seconds
- Height (h) is in meters

## Rearrangements:

- Solve for mass $m = \frac {gh}{GPE}$
- Solve for height $h = \frac {mg}{GPE}$
- Solve for gravity $g = \frac {mh}{GPE}$
