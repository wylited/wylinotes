# Kinetic Energy

$KE = \frac {mv^2}{2}$

## Where:

- Mass is in KG
- Velocity is in meters/second
- Kinetic Energy is in Joules

## Rearrangements:

- Solve for velocity $v = \sqrt \frac {KE}{\frac {m} {2}}$
- Solve for mass $m = \frac {2KE}{v^2}$
