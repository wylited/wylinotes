# Liquid Pressure

## $$ x = \frac {Y}{z} $$ (Solving for x)

## Where:
- x is a, given in foo. (Scalar or Vector)
- Y is b, given in bar. (Scalar or Vector)
- z is c, given in foobar. (Scalar or Vector)
	
## Rearrangements:
- Solve for Y, $Y = xz$
- Solve for z, $z = \frac {Y}{z}$
